I am a postdoctoral research fellow on the ARC project Meaning in Action at the University of Melbourne under the supervision of Greg Restall. I completed my PhD in Philosophy at the University of Pittsburgh with Anil Gupta as my advisor.

My research is in philosophical logic (truth, proof theory, modal logic, relevant logic) and philosophy of language.

I organize the Melbourne Logic Seminar.
